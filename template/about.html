ABOUT US

In the short span of its existence, People Care Hospital has created its niche on the map of quality healthcare delivery in South India by touching nearly 2 million lives. Having earned the trust and loyalty of patients through the compassion of its caregivers and high-precision medical technology, the institution has emerged as the leading quaternary care facility in the region.

We are committed to delivering top-notch healthcare services and ensuring the well-being of our community.

At People Care Hospital, we strive to create a welcoming and safe environment for all our patients, focusing on both their physical and emotional health.
